## Google Shared Drive

You both have Edit access to the Google Drive folder below, which is for both the overall Group and also the discrete Units of Scouts & Venturers. We inherited this folder from many years ago and have continued to maintain it.

Talk to mark for drive link. 

The key structure of the folders is as follows:

**9th Caulfield Group Folder** — has some folders that the committee uses and also the details of the QStore in there.

**Activities 20nn** — these are the details of what was completed each year; they contain photos, programs and other key documents. Starting from 2022 there is a subfolder of Venturers & Scouts in each year; prior to that it was always Scouts.

**Australian Scout Medallion** — these are the previous applications we have done for ASM/ASA over the years.

**Jamboree 2021/22** — important documents we had as part of the 2021/22 jamboree in Elmore.

**Mindari — GlenEira Stonnington** — historical folder of the Mindaris that were on; I have started putting some more recent documents in there also to build it back up.

**Scout Achievement Chart** — this is where I stored the spreadsheet I use to print and put up on the hall for where Scouts were up to.

**TeamApp** — this is the document that we often put in emails for Welcome to new families and how to use TeamApp as a Parent & Youth.
